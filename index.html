The Beatles

The Beatles were an English Pop Rock band, which started in the late 1950's and early 1960's. They soon became very well known for their music. The Beatles consisted of four members; John Lennon, Paul McCartney, George Harrison, and Ringo Starr. They originated in Liverpool, United Kingdom, where they would play together at clubs and were soon recognized for their music. Later in the the years they had become known the most influential act of the rock era. Fun fact their band name was a combination of "Beetles" and "Beat."

Abbey Road

Abbey Road is the eleventh studio album by the English rock band the Beatles, released on 26 September 1969.

Let It Be

Let It Be is the twelfth and final studio album by the English rock band the Beatles. It was released on 8 May 1970.

HELP!

Help! is the fifth studio album by the English rock band the Beatles and the soundtrack to their film of the same name. It was released on 6 August 1965.
